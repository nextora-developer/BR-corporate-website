FAQ

Common Questions

Is Bukku suitable for small businesses?
Yes. Bukku is designed for SMEs, startups, and service businesses. It simplifies invoicing, expense tracking, and reporting without requiring accounting knowledge.
Do I need accounting knowledge to use Bukku?
No. Bukku is built to be user-friendly. Most business owners can manage invoices, expenses, and basic reports without accounting background.
Can Bukku handle SST in Malaysia?
Yes. Bukku supports SST calculations and reporting, helping you stay compliant with Malaysia tax requirements.
Can Bukku integrate with my system or payment gateway?
Yes. Bukku can be integrated with your business workflow, including payment gateways like Revenue Monster, and other systems via API or custom integration.
Do you provide setup and support?
Yes. BRIF Cloud provides full onboarding, system setup, training, and ongoing support to ensure your accounting runs smoothly.
