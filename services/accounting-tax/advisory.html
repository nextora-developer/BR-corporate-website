Advisory vs Consulting

While advisory and consulting are often used interchangeably, they serve different purposes and business needs.

Advisory Support

Advisory support focuses on providing ongoing guidance, clarification and insight to help business owners make informed decisions.

  • Decision-focused and situational
  • Covers compliance, structure and financial implications
  • Ongoing support as business circumstances change
  • Practical advice aligned with regulations and reality

Advisory answers the question: “What should I do, and what are the implications?”

Consulting Services

Consulting typically focuses on solving specific problems or implementing defined projects within a set scope.

  • Project-based or scope-defined
  • Often focused on processes, systems or implementation
  • Delivers recommendations or execution outcomes
  • Engagement usually ends after project completion

Consulting answers the question: “How do we execute or fix this specific issue?”

In many cases, businesses benefit from advisory support first — followed by consulting when execution or specialised projects are required.
