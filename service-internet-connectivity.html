Important
Notes

To ensure a smooth installation process, please take note of the following infrastructure and provider dependencies.

Coverage & Infrastructure

Coverage availability and installation timelines are strictly dependent on service provider availability and your building's existing fibre infrastructure.

Condo & Apartment Readiness

High-rise installations may require Management Office approval or confirmation of existing fibre port readiness before our team can proceed.

Scheduling Autonomy

Final installation dates are determined and subject to the service provider’s master schedule. We facilitate the coordination but do not control provider time-slots.
