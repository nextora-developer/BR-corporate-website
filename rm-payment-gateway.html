FAQ

Common Questions

Do you charge setup fees?
In most cases, there’s no setup fee. The final scope depends on your specific integration requirements and business type.
Are failed/cancelled payments charged?
Typically, fees apply only to successful captured payments. Failed or cancelled transactions are usually not charged.
How long does settlement take?
Settlement is usually T+1 or T+2 working days, depending on the payment method and your specific approval settings.
Can you integrate with Laravel / custom systems?
Yes. We can integrate seamlessly into Laravel, Node.js, or other custom stacks, including full implementation of secure webhook signature verification.
